Recipes

A few of my greatest creations to both make and enjoy.

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • Corn Shells
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Taco Seasoning

How To Prepare:

  1. Boil chicken for 1 hour
  2. Shred with fork
  3. Sautee in pain with oil and seasoning
  4. Dice onion and cilantro
  5. Add meat and diced cilantro and onion to a corn shell
  6. Garnish with ingredients of your choice

Ceviche

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of already cooked shrimp
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Jalepeno
  • 2 Lemons and Limes

How To Prepare:

  1. Thaw shrimp and dice into cubes
  2. Dice cilantro, onion and jalepeno
  3. Mix ingredients into a bowl
  4. Squeeze in lemon and limes
  5. Mix and add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Let sit in fridge for at least 1 hour

Chicken Black Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • Black Beans, 1 can
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Chili powder and cumin
  • 3 cans of tomato sauce

How To Prepare:

  1. Dice chicken and onion
  2. Sautee in a large pot
  3. When chicken is browned add tomato sauce and blank beans (rinsed)
  4. Add seasonings
  5. Let simmer for at least 1 hour
  6. Garnish with cilantro and ingredients of your choice

Tortilla Soup

Ingredients

  • Corn Chips
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • 2 cans of chicken broth
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • Black Beans, 1 can
  • 1/2 can of corn
  • Salt and Pepper

How To Prepare:

  1. Grill chicken and dice
  2. In a large pot mix broth, sauce, beans and corn
  3. Salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add cooked chicken and let simmer for at least 1 hour
  5. Serve and garnish with onion and cilantro
  6. Add lime, avocado, corn chips and cheese before eating

Ingredients

A little bit about my favorite ingredients to cook with and eat.

Cilantro

At first, cilantro was one of my most hated foods. It's generally known to have a "soapy" taste and I found that true in my first experiences with the herb. After trying it a few times in various recipes I began to acquire a taste for it and it eventually turned into my favorite ingredient.

When to use it:

  • In salsa
  • As a garnish
  • In soups and sandwiches

Tomatoes

Tomatoes have a long history and tie into my Italian heritage. For many years my family used fresh tomatoes to make sauce for pastas and other Italian dishes. I grown to enjoy tomatoes that way and on basically everything I eat.

When to use them:

  • When making tomato sauce
  • On sandwiches
  • In salsa
  • In salads

Chipotle Peppers

Most of my experience with Chipotles have been with canned or dried chipotle but the flavor it adds is great enough to work its way into my favorites if used correctly. It adds a smokey, spice to any dish.

When to use it:

  • When boiling chicken and a few dried chipotles
  • When making chili dice up a couple canned chipotles in adobo sauce

Lime

Lime adds a refreshing spark of citrus flavor to everything all your favorite meals.

When to use it:

  • Best on cooked chicken or fish
